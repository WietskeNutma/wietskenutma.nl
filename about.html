About

Currently I,

Research, develop and coördinate the Zoöp project at the Zoönomic Institute. Zoöp is an organisational model for cooperation between human and nonhuman life that safeguards the interests of all zoë (Greek for ‘life’).
•
Coördinate the Memory of the Designed landscape program at Nieuwe Instituut. This programme works towards a future-proof perspective on the archiving of garden and landscape architecture. This field currently lacks a clear repository for the archives that document its history.
•
Make and repair textiles, in an effort to provide an alternative to the fast-fashion industry and to make mending accessible and commonplace again.

Previously I,

Led and organised a conversation series titled ‘Soundtracks of the City’ as part of a program titled The New Academy. Together with Rosi Braidotti and Shay Kreuger we explored various social themes through the lens of popular music in order to create not only evenings full of music, but also as a way to dig up and share knowledge about music practices and their impact. You can find our writings on the evenings here.
•
Worked as a freelance graphic designer for clients such as Corps, Nest and other cultural organisations.
•
Taught and designed a graphic design course for preparatory year students at the Royal Academy of Art in Den Haag, The Netherlands.
•
Got my BA in Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art in Den Haag, The Netherlands. During my graduation year I studied the intersection of the infrastructure of our telecommunication system and human connection.
